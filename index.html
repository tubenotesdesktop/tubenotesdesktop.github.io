Powerful Features

Everything you need to organize your video knowledge

Video Notes

Take rich notes directly linked to YouTube videos. Timestamp-based annotations with full rich text editing powered by Tiptap.

Infinite Canvas

Visualize your ideas on an infinite whiteboard. Draw shapes, add markdown nodes, connect ideas with flows powered by React Flow.

Kanban Board

Organize tasks with drag-and-drop boards. Track watching progress, prioritize important videos, and manage your learning workflow.

6 Themes

Choose from Cyberpunk Dark, Navy, Light and Editorial variants. Brutalist design with bold colors and sharp edges. Fully customizable.

Local-First

Your data stays on your machine. SQLite database with Prisma ORM. No cloud dependency, no subscriptions. Full privacy, full control.

Cross-Platform

Built with Electron & Next.js. Runs natively on Windows, macOS and Linux. PWA support available for web browsers too.
